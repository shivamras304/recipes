Ingredients:
 - All the namkeens you can find 
 - Green chutney
 - Red chutney
 - Onions
 - Tomatoes
 - Lemons
 - Papdi
 - Anything else you'd like

Directions:
 1. Take all the namkeens into a bowl.

 2. Add onions, tomatoes, boiled potatoes,papdi etc.

 3. Add all the chutneys and lemons to taste.

 4. Garnish with coriander leaves.

 5. Eat ASAP.
